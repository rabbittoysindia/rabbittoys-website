Frequently Asked

The small print, plainly.

Can I skip a month or cancel anytime? +

Absolutely. You can skip, pause, swap, or cancel any month from your account — no questions, no penalties, no awkward calls.

How are toys selected for my child? +

When you subscribe, you tell us your child's age, favourite themes, and any toys they already own. Our curator builds each month's box by hand, ensuring no overlap and steady developmental progression.

Do you ship outside India? +

Currently we ship across India only. International shipping is on our 2026 roadmap — join our newsletter to be the first to know.

What's your return policy? +

15-day no-questions-asked returns on single purchases. For subscription boxes, you can return any item within the box for a full credit on your next month.

Is everything safe for young children? +

Yes. Every toy meets BIS/ISI safety standards. We label each piece with a recommended age range and never include small parts for the under-3 collection.
