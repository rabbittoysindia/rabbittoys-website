The story behind the story.

A small workshop, a big belief — that toys should be built for memory, not just for moments.

Est. 2018, Pune

It began with one wooden rabbit.

RabbitToys was born when our founder, a new parent, couldn't find a toy that felt worthy of her daughter's first birthday. Plastic, loud, disposable — they all felt forgettable. So she made one herself: a small wooden rabbit, sanded smooth, painted in safe pigments, signed on the underside.

That rabbit still sits on her daughter's shelf today. And it started everything.

Eight years later, we curate over 200 pieces from artisans across India and a handful of trusted partners abroad. Each one is chosen by hand, packed with care, and shipped with a small note — because we believe a toy isn't just a toy. It's the first object that teaches a child what beauty feels like.

— with love, the RabbitToys family

Our Promise

In an age of endless plastic and forgettable trends, we believe in the opposite. We choose slowly. We test relentlessly. We say no to most things — so that the few that earn a place in our collection deserve a place in your home.
